About Me

Welcome to my page!

My name is Nasib, Founder and CEO of “Keep Nasib Happy!” An early stage, yet scalable and highly profitable, imaginary company that has massively grown in size and areas of expertise during the last couple of months.

“Keep Nasib Happy” offers individual analysis and personal trainings and provides constant and enormous support to its very own client which is myself! (surprise surprise!)

Shout out to the efforts and sleepless nights of this company, I was able to deeply dig into my soul and add missing values, meanings and colors to my journey of life. And, for the last couple of months have been able to step into the beautiful path of consciously living my everyday life as if it is my last.

Here are bunch of realizations and goals that “Keep Nasib Happy” has provided me and I must or at least try to respect them on a daily basis.

  • I need to simply get my hands dirty and create new things; I just do!

    Whether it is an intimate dance with the colors on a painting pallet, or flirting with a piece of simple or sophisticated code that introduces beautiful, stunning, new products to the world; My soul feeds off it and gets drunk on it.

    Check out the "Portfolio" tab to see some of my art and design works.

  • In this age of internet, social media, easy access and constant distraction, I need to learn being focused and respectful of my time. Need to prioritize my daily activities and say “yes” only to the things that extremely excite me or add a meaningful value to my soul or life. The choice is clear; It’s either “no” or “HELL YESSS!”

  • I need to do a bit of meditation on a daily basis, be aware of my mind and thoughts, and exercise the muscle of making peace with the world.

  • I need to simply be grateful for all the precious things that world has offered me, the health, the loving family, great people that I have been lucky enough to meet and grow among, the long path that I went through to get to this moment in life and all the little achievements that I have made along the way. Being grateful and consciously saying it outload is an instant injection of happiness hormon to my brain.

  • Ego is a quick way of self-fooling and is not welcome in my world. It closes the doors of communication, and kills the learning and growing opportunities, which is itself a direct path to unhappiness.

  • The last, but not the least: laughing at world and being simply happy is a choice!

Make me laugh, teach me something, help me learn how to make true peace with the world, and as a result:

Win a position in my imaginary first-class company!
